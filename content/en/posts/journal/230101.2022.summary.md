## Year in Brief

**January**

Enrolled in a drawing class and learned some basic sketching. Unfortunately, I didn't keep it up.

Seriously considered making a move toward work I'd actually enjoy — sent out a résumé and landed an offer for a physics simulation job. I was planning to jump ship after the New Year.

**February**

Lupin III Season 6.

Found a clip of myself making an incredible play in Overwatch.

Shenzhen began mandatory routine PCR testing.

After watching *Now You See Me* and *Cinderella at Midnight*, I ordered a deck of cards online and learned a few basic magic tricks. Unfortunately, I didn't keep that up either.

**March**

My manager called me in for a chat — there was an opportunity to go to Russia. But I'd already accepted the new offer.

I agonized over it for a while.

…In the end, I decided to pass on the job switch and go to Russia instead.

It felt a bit like going with the flow, but going with the flow is still a choice I made.

Switched to a red iPhone 13 and sold my old 8 Plus to a forum friend.

**April**

Gave away or sold most of my belongings.

Had a wisdom tooth pulled.

**May**

Participated in the Overwatch 2 beta — got a pentakill with Genji 😄

Prepared a short-term visa, took a business English exam, and flew Shenzhen → Beijing → Moscow → Novosibirsk.

![](https://pic.aspi-rin.top/2025/08/3d99a55b4174a0da3d6df213b197efd5.jpg)

Met a forum friend in Beijing; at midnight we were eating McFlurries in the hotel watching naked men wrestle.

**June & July**

Enjoyed the brief Novosibirsk summer between work.

![](https://pic.aspi-rin.top/2025/08/79605d5586d25eb277ac6fa3687aa531.jpg)

**August**

Returned home, quarantined, and tested positive for COVID on the second day in the hotel…

Experienced the full gamut of China's pandemic response procedures firsthand.

**September**

Discharged from hospital, home quarantine, returned to Shenzhen for work, and started the work visa process.

![](https://pic.aspi-rin.top/2025/08/05f6a430de85b9e850c151c99ea4f918.jpg)

Dinner with forum friends.

**October**

OW1 shut down — six years of random-hero duo queues 😎

![](https://pic.aspi-rin.top/2025/08/7258a88dbfde4102b60705d7e27cb367.jpg)

OW2 launched.

Moved out of the Shenzhen apartment: Shenzhen → Moscow → Novosibirsk. Played mahjong in Russia.

Novosibirsk already had snow.

**November**

Handled paperwork in Moscow, rented a 100-square-meter cabin in the forest near the Novosibirsk research institute, and received a refund for a flight cancelled due to market circuit breakers.

![My mansion in the forest](https://pic.aspi-rin.top/2024/02/59fdabc2b0670b3644aaacb1de9e7b14.jpeg)

My mansion in the forest.

**December**

China lifted its zero-COVID policy.

Received my first euro-denominated paycheck, went with colleagues to a nearby sanatorium on the weekend, and exchanged Christmas gifts.

## Books, Films & More

Didn't read much this year. Since my home internet struggled to load certain images on Douban, I'm skipping the collage and just picking one favorite in each category.

**Novel:** *The Tatami Time Machine Blues* — pickings were so slim this year I had to go with the tallest dwarf.

**Film:** *Bullet Train* — I read the original novel ages ago; it was fun.

**Anime:** *Lupin III Part 6*, Episode 4 — the overall series felt average, but this standalone episode really got me.

**Game:** *AI: The Somnium Files – nirvanA Initiative* — bought the sequel, then played the dusty original first. Sequels are usually a step down, but this one's story held up.
